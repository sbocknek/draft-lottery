Overview

Previous-year finishers 12th through 8th auto-qualify for the lottery.

Their default odds of winning the lottery are 39%, 28%, 18%, 10%, and 5%.

Every manager gets 2% for each trade made the previous year.

Trade odds are calculated and locked in first as actual percentages.

The default odds (12th through 8th) are then scaled proportionally.
